Game Rules

A small word puzzle game that presents you a mangled word asking you to enter the correct, unmangled word. If the word to unmangle is banana the game may present the word as aaabnn and you must then enter b-a-n-a-n-a in this order for the solution to be accepted.

A game runs for 40 seconds, during which you can solve as many words as you can manage. Everytime you hit the delete key, the score you'll earn for solving that specific word decrease. Only the top 10 make it to the high score ranking.
